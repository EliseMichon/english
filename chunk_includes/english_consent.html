Please consider this information carefully before deciding to participate in our research:

We invite you to participate in a 20-minute research study on language production and comprehension. We will ask you to do a linguistic task such as reading sentences or words, hearing sounds or words, naming pictures or describing scenes.

There are no risks or benefits of any kind involved in this study. If you have read this form and have decided to participate in this experiment, please understand that your participation is voluntary and that you have the right to withdraw your consent or discontinue your participation at any time.

Note that the results are sent to us only at the end of the experiment, when you validate the final form. We ask that you do the experiment only once, even if you are unable to complete it in one session, to keep all participants equal with regards to their participation in the task.

Your participation in this study will remain confidential. Your data will be automatically anonymized. Your individual privacy will be maintained in all published and written data resulting from the study. You may print this form for your records.
